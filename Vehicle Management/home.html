Vehicle Management System!

What is the problem?

From northeast the vehicles are being stolen often and illegally transfered to other states. The police at that area is unable to recognise the stolen vehicle because there are too many ways to hide the identity of the vehicle.

What is the solution?

There will be a Vehicle Identification Number(VIN) which will be printed on different parts of a vehicle.The Police and authorized officials can use this number to have access to the details of the vehicle.
